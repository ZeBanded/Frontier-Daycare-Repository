# Efficient Level Grinding in Scarlet/Violet

## Chansey/Blissey farm

Travel to North Province (Area 3), where the Fairy-type Team Star base is.

![](/images/sv/north-province-3.png)

Make one of the below sandwiches, and Level 45 Chansey and Blissey will spawn in large numbers!

![](/images/sv/exp-chansey.png)

### Sandwich options

#### Creative Mode #1

This gives both Encounter Power: Normal Lv. 1 and Exp. Point Power: Normal Lv. 1.

```
Custom Sandwich ⭐⭐⭐

Ingredients:
Top baguette has been dropped
4x Tofu, 2x Watercress (drop 1), 2x Pepper, 2x Salt

Effects:
Exp. Point Power: Normal - Lv. 1
Raid Power: Flying - Lv. 1
Encounter Power: Normal - Lv. 1
```

#### Creative Mode #2

Use one Tofu (can drop one piece) and one Salt for Encounter Power: Normal Lv. 1.

```
Custom Sandwich ⭐⭐⭐

Ingredients:
Top baguette has been dropped
1x Tofu, 1x Salt

Effects:
Encounter Power: Normal - Lv. 1
Raid Power: Psychic - Lv. 1
Exp. Point Power: Electric - Lv. 1
```

#### Recipe Mode #34

The Ultra Curry-and-Rice-Style Sandwich gives Encounter Power: Normal Lv. 2.

![](/images/sv/sandwich-34.png)
