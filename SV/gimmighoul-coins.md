# Farming Gimmighoul Coins

> [!NOTE]
> Gholdengo is not yet usable in Pokemon Champions. This guide is still provided for posterity.

## Scarlet/Violet

First, if it's been a decent while (more than a few days), go through your game and grab the Coins from as many Roaming and Chest Gimmighoul as you can find. Serebii.net's [Paldea Pokearth](https://www.serebii.net/pokearth/paldea/) has separate toggles for Roaming and Chest Gimmighoul.

[Sending a Postcard from Pokemon GO](/GO/connect-sv.md) will also net 50-100 Coins. This can be done once a day.

Don't worry about overstocking. An NPC in Medali will pick up excess collected Coins, [up to over four billion](https://x.com/Sibuna_Switch/status/1793314074370912664). [Video clip showing how to get to the NPC from the west Pokemon Center.](/images/sv/medali-coin-npc-location.mp4)

### Dateskipping

It's possible to dateskip for a chance to respawn Gimmighoul right away.

First, look for the Gimmighoul you want to focus on respawning. Roaming Gimmighoul is recommended, as it has a 50% chance of respawning at least one, versus Chest Gimmighoul which has a 5% chance of respawning *only* one.

The southern Pokemon Center in Cascarrafa is a good choice, as the Asado Desert Watchtower isn't that far away and has a second Roaming Gimmighoul. The steps below will use these locations, though there may be a more efficient set of fast travel points elsewhere.

![](/images/sv/map-cascarrafa-south.jpg)

![](/images//sv/gimmighoul-cascarrafa.jpg)

1. Ensure you have the fast travel points for the southern Pokemon Center in Cascarrafa and the Asado Desert Watchtower.
2. Collect the Roaming Gimmighoul at the aforementioned locations.
3. Save the game at either spot.
4. Go to your Switch's Date and Time settings and skip forward one day.
5. Close and reopen the game to advance the in-game time.
6. Warp to the other fast travel point, check the Gimmighoul there.
7. Warp back, check the Gimmighoul there.
8. Repeat steps 3-7 until you've had enough.

Remember, there is a 50% chance for Roaming Gimmighoul to respawn *at all*, so don't be put off if they don't respawn. Continue following the steps above, regardless of whether any appear.

This can be [automated](https://pokemonautomation.github.io/Programs/PokemonSV/GimmighoulRoamingFarmer.html).
