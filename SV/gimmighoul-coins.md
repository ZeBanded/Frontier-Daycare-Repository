# Farming Gimmighoul Coins in Scarlet/Violet

Serebii.net's [Paldea Pokearth](https://www.serebii.net/pokearth/paldea/) has toggles for Roaming and Chest Gimmighoul.

It's [evidently](https://pokemonautomation.github.io/Programs/PokemonSV/GimmighoulChestFarmer.html) [possible](https://pokemonautomation.github.io/Programs/PokemonSV/GimmighoulRoamingFarmer.html) to dateskip to respawn Gimmighoul, but this isn't very well documented.

An NPC in Medali will pick up excess Coins, up to an absurd amount (over four billion): https://x.com/Sibuna_Switch/status/1793314074370912664
