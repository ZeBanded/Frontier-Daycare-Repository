# Transferring from Pokemon GO to Pokemon HOME

This guide shows how to transfer Pokemon caught in Pokemon GO, to Pokemon HOME.

You do *not* need Nintendo Switch Online or Pokemon HOME Premium.

Once you transfer a Pokemon to Pokemon HOME, it can *never* return to GO.

## Sending from Pokemon GO

Open Pokemon GO. Go to the Settings menu, then select "Connected Devices and Services" -> "Pokemon HOME".

![](/images/go/settings.png)
![](/images/go/connected-devices-1.png)

![](/images/go/connected-devices-2.png)

If you haven't yet connected Pokemon GO to Pokemon HOME, follow the instructions to do so. Otherwise, tap "Send Pokemon".

![](/images/go/send-pokemon.png)

Take note of your remaining GO Transporter Energy. It caps at 10,000 points, and refills over the span of a week, limiting how often you can send Pokemon. While regular Pokemon won't impact it very much, Shiny, Legendary, and Mythical Pokemon will heavily drain your GO Transporter Energy. If your Pokemon is one of the original 151, an Alolan form, Meltan, or Melmetal, you may want to instead [transfer to Let's Go! Pikachu/Eevee](/GO/transfer-lgpe.md) if possible.

It isn't recommended to power up or evolve your Pokemon before sending to HOME, unless you don't have any main series games. This otherwise wastes Stardust and Candy, and higher-leveled Pokemon use more GO Transporter Energy.

![](/images/go/transporter-energy.png)

Tap Continue. Select up to 25 Pokemon to transfer at once by tapping them, then tap Next. Ensure you're transferring the right Pokemon, then tap Transport.

![](/images/go/transfer-select.png)
![](/images/go/transfer-confirm.png)
![](/images/go/transfer-anim.png)
![](/images/go/transfer-complete.png)

Some Pokemon can't be transferred, notably including Shadow Pokemon (purify them first). See [here](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/GO_Transporter#Restrictions) for an updated list.

## Claiming in Pokemon HOME

Now, we will need to claim our Pokemon in Pokemon HOME. You can use either the Switch or mobile version, it doesn't matter.

Regardless of how you claim them, they will be placed in the first available box spaces in HOME.

### Nintendo Switch version

Upon logging in, you will be informed that Pokemon have been transferred.

![](/images/home-switch/go-transfer-nag.jpg)

On the main menu, select "Pokemon GO" (it will be marked with a red dot), then follow the instructions.

![](/images/home-switch/go-transfer-menu.jpg)

![](/images/home-switch/go-transfer-confirm.jpg)

### Mobile version

Upon logging in, you will be informed that Pokemon have been transferred.

![](/images/home-mobile/go-transfer-nag.png)

Tap "Yes", which will take you to the Options page. Then tap "Receive" under "Pokemon GO Link".

If you accidentally tap "No" on the initial prompt, navigate to the Options by going to the "Your Room" tab on the main screen, then tapping the button at the bottom. The red exclamation mark will guide you.

![](/images/home-mobile/options-go.png)
![](/images/home-mobile/go-transfer-1.png)
![](/images/home-mobile/go-transfer-2.png)
