# Farm Peat Blocks and Black Augurite in Legends: Arceus

This guide shows how to easily and repeatedly get Peat Blocks and Black Augurite in Pokemon Legends: Arceus.

1. Decide which evolution item you're going for, as this determines which map you should go to. Peat Blocks are more likely to be found in Mirelands, Coastlands, or Highlands, while Black Augurite are more likely to be found in Grasslands and Coastlands.
2. Load the relevant map and go to an Ursaluna dig spot that is close to a subarea, like the Lake Valor cave in Mirelands.
3. Dig and hope for the Peat Block / Black Augurite. If you don't get it, go back to Jubilife and reload until you do get it. This may take some time.
4. Once you dig up the evolution item, enter the nearby subarea.
5. Wait three minutes in the subarea.
6. Leave the subarea and go back to the same dig spot, and you will find the evolution item again!
7. Repeat steps 3-6 until you decide you have enough of the evolution item.

You could stay in the area, transfer in Ursaring/Scyther from other games, and dedicate your save file to evolving that Pokemon for a while.
