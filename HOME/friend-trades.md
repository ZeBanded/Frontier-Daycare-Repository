# Using Friend Trades in Pokemon HOME

This guide shows how to directly trade with others in Pokemon HOME using Friend Trades.

This is a good option if you don't have any Switch games or Nintendo Switch Online, as it requires *no subscriptions* to use. Also, since this is a direct trade, it's impossible for your trade to be sniped. However, as a tradeoff, you can only make ten Friend Trades per day. For this reason, it is often recommended to use the [GTS](/HOME/gts.md) instead, which has the same benefits.

**NOTE:** If your Nintendo account is marked as less than 16 years of age, you will not be able to add friends or do Friend Trades. In this case, you will need to use the [GTS](/HOME/gts.md) or a Switch game. There is no way around this without violating Nintendo's terms of service.

## Adding your trade partner as a friend

1. If you haven't already, download the mobile version of Pokemon HOME from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, and sign into the same Nintendo account you use, or plan to use, on your Switch and/or Pokemon Champions.

2. If you're looking to receive a competitive Pokemon, ensure you have a junk Pokemon to trade, like Fletchling. If you have a Switch game, deposit a Pokemon into HOME. Otherwise, you can [transfer a Pokemon from Pokemon GO](/GO/transfer-home.md).

3. On the main screen, where you see your name and/or a Pokemon, tap "Friends".

![](/images/home-mobile/your-room-friends.png)

4. Tap "Add Friend" at the top of the screen.

![](/images/home-mobile/friends.png)

5. Take note of your Friend Code.

![](/images/home-mobile/friend-code.png)

6. Follow *one* of the following subsections.

### Sending your Friend Code to your trade partner

1. Make sure you are still on the screen with your Friend Code.
2. Tap "Copy Code". This will copy your Friend Code to your clipboard.
3. Send the copied Friend Code to your trade partner. They will enter it on their end.
4. Tap the X button at the bottom of the screen until you have returned to the main screen, where you see your name and/or a Pokemon.
5. Wait for a red exclamation mark icon to appear on the "Friends" button. If it doesn't appear after a while, double check with your trade partner, and make sure you sent the code correctly.

![](/images/home-mobile/your-room-friends-notif.png)

6. Tap the "Friends" button.
7. Tap your trade partner in Pokemon HOME and accept the friend request.

### Entering your trade partner's Friend Code

1. Make sure you are still on the screen with your Friend Code.
2. Copy your trade partner's Friend Code to your clipboard.
3. Tap "Tap here and enter a Friend Code".
4. Tap the text box, then paste the Friend Code.
5. Tap Confirm.
6. If successful, your trade partner will receive a friend request.

## Starting a Friend Trade

1. From the main screen, where you see your name and/or a Pokemon, swipe to the right. You will end up on the "Trade" tab.
2. Note the number next to "Friend Trade". This is the number of trades you have left for the day. If it says 0, you must use the [GTS](/HOME/gts.md), use a Switch game, or wait until tomorrow.

![](/images/home-mobile/trade-friend-trade.png)

3. Tap "Friend Trade". Your friends list will appear.
4. Tap your trade partner.
5. Wait for your trade partner to follow steps 1-4 themselves, if they haven't already. Your Pokemon list will appear.
6. If you are the one receiving a competitive Pokemon, select a random junk Pokemon like Fletchling. If you are the one sending a competitive Pokemon, select it.
7. Press the orange "Ready!" button to confirm the trade.

![](/images/home-mobile/trade-ready.png)

8. Wait for your trade partner to also hit the "Ready!" button, if they haven't already.
