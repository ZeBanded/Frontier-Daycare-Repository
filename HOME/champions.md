# Sending Pokemon to Champions

In the main menu of Pokemon Home, select the tab called Pokemon Champions

![](/images/home-switch/champions-home-menu-tab.png)

If you have an active Pokemon Champions file on the Switch account, you will be shown your trainer name and support ID.

![](/images/home-switch/Home-Champions-Account.jpg)

Select the Pokemon from your Pokemon Home box, and they will be sent to Pokemon Champions as a Visitor.

![](/images/home-switch/Home-Champions-Box.jpg)

While a Pokemon is visiting, it:
- cannot be traded in Home
- will take up a box space in Home
- will take up a box space in Champions

In Pokemon Champions, in the recruit menu, select "Collect from Pokemon Home", then receive your visitor Pokemon

![](/images/Champions/Champions-Ranch-Collect-from-home.jpg)
![](/images/Champions/Champions-Ranch-Home-Box.jpg)
